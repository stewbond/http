About Ferguson Engineering

Ferguson Engineering is a new company incorporated in 2016, dedicated to continuing to advance the flight training simulation industry through superior engineering. Though our organization may be new, our members have significant experience with mulitple training device manufacturers (TDMs) and are significantly involved in the industry.

We have a multitude of services within our expertise which we can offer. Our technical abilities make us effective at solving difficult technical issues, or designing and developing complicated systems. We can also provide on-site support during installations. Do you have an engineering need? If so, contact us.
