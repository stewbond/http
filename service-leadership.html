Leadership

Our members have significant experience leading challenging technical projects through to project completion. We can not only provide technical subject matter expertise (SME) to your engineers, but we can also guide those engineers through the excecution, establishing productive business rhythms, individual accountability, and a "let's do this" team atmosphere. In addition to day-to-day activities, we can critique design reviews, establish plans, recognize risks and opportunities, and design earned value metrics (EVM). EVM will help you to understand the cost and schedule performance of your projects, and will help the drive the team to remain on-plan, on-schedule and on-cost. We can help you to mitigate your risks and realize your opportunities.

If you need a technical lead, a second-pair of eyes to review your planning/execution, or simply an additional person to help you re-plan your project through a difficult or busy period we can accommodate you.
